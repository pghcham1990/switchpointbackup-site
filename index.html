Controls that keep costs predictable

The backup connection is intentionally constrained. This is not a second full-time internet circuit. The goal is business continuity during ISP outages with predictable behavior and clear approval steps.

A

Monthly data cap

Example: 1 GB per month of failover data. Typically enough to keep a small team operational during a short ISP outage.

B

Hard stop, no surprise bills

When the LTE cap is reached, the backup connection stops. No automatic overages. You choose whether to authorize more data or wait.

C

Add more only with your approval

If the outage continues and you want additional LTE capacity, we add it on demand. We never add charges without your explicit authorization.

What we manage: the failover device, SIM, monitoring, alerts, and the traffic policies. What we do not do: we do not replace your ISP or provide primary internet service.
