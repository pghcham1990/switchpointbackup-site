FAQ

Do I need a new internet provider

No. SwitchPoint sits on top of your existing provider. We add automatic LTE failover so you stay online when your ISP has an outage.

Do I need a static IP

No for most businesses. This service is designed for outbound connectivity during outages. If you host on site services that require inbound access, we can review your setup.

Will it work during a power outage

If the building has no power, network gear will go down. LTE failover protects against ISP outages while power is on. For brief power outages, a small UPS can keep the modem and device running.

How fast is failover

Most environments fail over within about 10 to 30 seconds. We test failover during installation.
