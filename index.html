Why this business exists

Most businesses do not realize how many systems quietly depend on internet access until it is gone. Outages create a chain reaction: transactions fail, phones degrade, scheduling stalls, and staff time gets burned on troubleshooting. SwitchPoint Backup is built to reduce that downtime without turning into a second full time internet service.

1

Outages are not rare

Fiber, cable, DSL, and even modern wireless can go down. Weather, construction, hardware failure, upstream issues, it all happens.

2

Downtime is expensive

The cost is not only internet. It is staff time, missed revenue, delayed care or service, and frustrated customers.

3

Continuity needs discipline

Failover must be controlled. The goal is to keep operations online, not to encourage replacing the primary ISP with a backup link.

The idea in one sentence: your internet should have a spare tire that is ready instantly, predictable in behavior, and managed by someone accountable.

FAQ

Are you an internet provider

No. We do not replace your existing internet service. You keep your current provider. We deploy and manage failover equipment that provides continuity when your primary link is unavailable.

Will this work with my current provider

Yes. The service is designed to sit on top of common primary connections such as fiber, cable, DSL, and other wired internet services. We can also support more advanced designs depending on your environment.

Is this secure

The failover device uses enterprise networking principles and can apply standard firewalling, segmentation, and policy controls. Your traffic remains your traffic. The service focuses on continuity and managed operation, not data collection.

Will my staff have to do anything during an outage

No. That is the point. The system detects a failure and switches automatically. When the primary connection returns, it switches back.

How do you prevent the backup from becoming “internet #2”

The solution is designed around operational continuity with controls. It is engineered to be used during outages, not as a replacement for your primary ISP.

Do you deploy outside Pennsylvania

Yes. The service is straightforward to deploy and can be implemented nationally. We can start local and expand as operational demand grows.
