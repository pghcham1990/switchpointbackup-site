We Install a Small Device

      
We place a Peplink Balance One next to your existing router. It plugs into your internet and adds a dedicated LTE cellular connection as a backup. The install takes about an hour, and your team won't notice a thing — business runs as normal the entire time.

    

    

      
It Watches Your Connection 24/7

      
Our monitoring system checks your internet connection every few seconds — around the clock. We don't wait for you to call and tell us something's wrong. We already know. If your ISP goes down at 2 AM, our system detects it before your first employee even arrives.

Internet Drops? You Stay Online.

      
The moment your ISP fails, the device detects it and switches your entire business to the LTE backup in under 30 seconds. Credit card machines, phones, email, cloud apps — everything keeps running. Most of the time, your team won't even realize anything happened.

    

    

      
We Handle Everything

      
You don't manage the device. You don't update firmware. You don't troubleshoot. We monitor it, we maintain it, we handle carrier relationships, and we send you a report if anything happens. You focus on running your business — we make sure you can.

    

    

      
One Simple Monthly Fee

      
No surprise bills. No per-incident charges. One predictable monthly cost that protects your entire operation. Every plan includes the hardware, installation, monitoring, management, and support.
